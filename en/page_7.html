Heat Pump vs Electric Boiler vs Ion Heating Boiler Demonstration Test Proposal

Demonstration testing plan for comparing performance and efficiency of three heating systems

Option 1: Energy Systems Catapult

Living Lab & WESA

Purpose: Compare performance and user response of 3 technologies in real home environments

Scope:

• Pilot installation in select homes from 5,500 households across the UK

Measurement Items:

  • Power consumption (kWh)
  • Heating response speed and temperature maintenance capability
  • User convenience and installation ease
  • Carbon emission reduction effects

Option 2: University of Salford

Energy House 2.0

Purpose: Precise performance comparison under extreme cold/heat and various weather conditions

Method:

• Cross-testing of 3 heating systems under identical home conditions

Measurement Items:

  • Energy efficiency (heating output vs power consumption)
  • Extreme low temperature (-20°C) performance
  • Temperature control precision
  • Installation space and maintenance requirements per system
