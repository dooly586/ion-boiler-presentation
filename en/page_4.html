KCL Official Test

Ion Heating Boiler objectively verified its performance through official demonstration tests by Korea Conformity Laboratories (KCL).

Test Conditions

  • Outdoor temperature: -12.5℃ ~ +5℃
  • Indoor temperature: Maintained at 25℃
  • Comparison target: Mitsubishi Inverter Heat Pump (COP 3.2)

These test results clearly demonstrate that Ion Heating Boiler has superior energy efficiency and carbon reduction effects compared to existing heat pumps.

Key Findings

  • Ion Heating Boiler achieved 28.1% reduction in power consumption.
  • Carbon emissions were 43% reduced.
  • Temperature control accuracy: ±0.1℃
  • Heating speed: Approximately 2x faster than conventional electric boilers
