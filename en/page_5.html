Technology Introduction - Ion Heating Boiler

Operating Principle

Ion Heating Boiler operates on a unique principle that is differentiated from conventional indirect heating methods.

Generates heat through ionic friction by directly applying alternating current to water, utilizing the frictional motion of ions present in water.

This utilizes the principle of 'water molecular friction heat', where water molecules and electrons move actively to directly generate thermal energy.

Minimal heat loss and very high efficiency.

Conversion Efficiency

99.6%

Published in Physics Society Paper Highlight

Core Technology Features

Fast Heating Speed

Achieves required temperature quickly with approximately 2x faster heating speed compared to conventional electric boilers.

Precise Temperature Control

Enables precise temperature control of ±0.1℃, minimizing energy waste and providing stable temperature maintenance.

Eco-friendly & Silent

No carbon emissions or fine dust generation due to no combustion process, and virtually silent operation with electronic heating method.

Easy Installation

Easy installation without outdoor unit, refrigerant, or chimney, and compact size at 1/6 the level of similar models enhances space utilization.

Key Features Summary

Ion Heating Boiler is more than just a heating device; it's an innovative heating solution that maximizes energy efficiency and dramatically reduces carbon emissions. This will be a key alternative for a sustainable future.
